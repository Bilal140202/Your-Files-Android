Privacy Policy

Your Files — File Manager for Android

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Your Files does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All file operations are performed entirely on your device. No internet connection is required for any feature.

1. Overview

Your Files is a local file management application designed for Android devices. The app allows users to browse, manage, organize, clean, and optimize files stored on their device. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data remains entirely under your control.

This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data handling, the permissions the app requires, and how your information is (or is not) processed. By using Your Files, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

Your Files does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not include any analytics, advertising, telemetry, or tracking SDKs. Specifically, the app does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, advertising ID)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Files, file contents, or file metadata
  • Browsing history or user behavior patterns
  • Network information or IP addresses

No data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third-party service at any time.

3. Permissions and Data Access

Your Files requires the following Android permissions to function. Each permission is used exclusively for on-device operations:

  • Storage / File Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required to browse, read, move, copy, rename, and delete files on your device. All file operations are performed locally. No file contents or metadata are transmitted anywhere.
  • Media Storage (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO, READ_MEDIA_AUDIO): Required on Android 13+ to access media files (photos, videos, audio) stored on your device for categorization, duplicate detection, and cleanup features.
  • Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required on Android 13+ to display notifications for background operations such as file deletion progress or image optimization completion.

4. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • User Preferences: Theme settings (dark/light mode, color theme), and other user preferences. Stored using Android SharedPreferences.
  • Trash Records: Information about files moved to the in-app trash (original path, trash path, deletion timestamp) to enable the restore and undo features. Stored using Android SharedPreferences.
  • File Database Cache: A local Room database used to cache file listings for faster browsing performance.

All locally stored data is managed entirely by the app and is not accessible to any other application or external entity. Users can clear all app data at any time through Android Settings > Apps > Your Files > Storage > Clear Data.

5. Third-Party Services

Your Files does not integrate with any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks (Google AdMob, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services (Firebase Crashlytics, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs
  • No payment processors
  • No cloud storage services

The app uses the following open-source libraries for on-device functionality only:

  • AndroidX Jetpack Compose — UI framework
  • Coil — Local image loading
  • ExoPlayer — Local media playback
  • Room — Local database

These libraries operate entirely on-device and do not transmit any data externally.

6. Data Sharing

Your Files does not share, sell, rent, or otherwise distribute any user data to third parties. Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to share.

The app does include standard Android sharing functionality (Share Sheet) which allows users to manually share files with other apps of their choosing. This is user-initiated sharing and is not monitored or intercepted by Your Files.

7. Children's Privacy

Your Files is suitable for users of all ages. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Since no data is collected, transmitted, or shared, the app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Family Policy requirements.

8. Data Security

Since Your Files operates entirely offline and does not transmit data, there is minimal risk of data interception. All data remains on your device and is protected by Android's built-in security model, including:

  • Android sandboxing — the app can only access files within its designated storage areas and those explicitly granted by the user
  • Scoped Storage enforcement on Android 10+
  • Permission-based access controls

9. User Rights and Control

You have full control over your data at all times. You can:

  • Revoke storage permissions at any time via Android Settings
  • Clear all app data via Android Settings > Apps > Your Files > Storage > Clear Data
  • Uninstall the app to remove all app-related data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. If the app introduces any new features that involve data collection (which we have no plans for), we will update this policy accordingly before releasing such features.

11. Google Play Data Safety

The following declarations are made in the Google Play Data Safety section:

  • Data collected: None
  • Data shared with third parties: None
  • Data transferred: None (all processing is local/on-device)
  • Data deleted: Users can delete all app data via Android Settings at any time
