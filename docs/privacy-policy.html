Privacy Policy for Your Files

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Introduction

Your Files ("the app") is a privacy-first file manager application for Android devices, developed by Bilal Ahmed ("the developer"). This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Your Files does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not require any user accounts, registration, or internet connection to function.

  • No personal information is collected or stored by the app.
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No advertising SDKs are integrated into the app.
  • No third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) are used.

App Permissions

Your Files requests the following Android permissions to function:

  • Storage / Files and Media Access — Required to browse, manage, copy, move, rename, and delete files on your device. The app only accesses files you explicitly interact with.
  • Notification Permission — Used solely for background file operation notifications (e.g., when a copy or move operation completes).

All file operations (read, write, delete, move, copy) are performed locally on your device. No file content is ever uploaded or transmitted to any server.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • User preferences — Theme selection and dark mode preference (stored in Android SharedPreferences).
  • File metadata cache — A local Room database caches file metadata (names, sizes, dates) and duplicate detection hashes to improve performance. This data never leaves your device.
  • Recycle bin — Deleted files are temporarily moved to the app's private storage before permanent deletion. This data is stored only on your device.

Clearing the app's data or uninstalling it will remove all locally stored preferences and cached metadata. Your actual files are never modified by clearing app data.

Network Communication

Your Files is designed to work entirely offline. The app does not initiate any network connections for its core file management functionality.

The only exception is the Privacy Policy link in the Settings page, which opens this page in your device's web browser. This requires a standard web browser intent and does not involve any data transmission from the app itself.

Third-Party Services

Your Files does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Your Files does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require age verification and does not collect personal data from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
